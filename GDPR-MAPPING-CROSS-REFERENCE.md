**Document ID:** PIMS-SUP-010  
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025-01-01  
**Owner:** DPO | **Classification:** Public

## 1. Purpose

This cross-reference maps ISO/IEC 27701:2019 clauses and controls to the corresponding GDPR articles. It helps organisations using this toolkit identify which toolkit documents support their GDPR compliance obligations, and enables auditors to quickly locate evidence against GDPR requirements.
