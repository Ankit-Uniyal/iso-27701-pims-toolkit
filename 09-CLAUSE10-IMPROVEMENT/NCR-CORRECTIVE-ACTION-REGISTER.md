## 1. Purpose

This register records all Non-Conformities (NCs) identified within the PIMS, together with root cause analysis, corrective actions, and effectiveness verification. It supports ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Clause 10.1.

---

## 2. NC Sources

NCs may be identified from: Internal PIMS audits, external/certification audits, regulatory investigations, privacy incidents and breach investigations, management reviews, self-assessments, complaints from data subjects, staff-reported concerns, or supplier non-conformances.

---

## 3. NC Classification

| Classification | Description | Response Timeline |
|---------------|------------|-----------------|
| **Major NC** | Systematic failure; risk of regulatory breach | 30 days for plan; 90 days for closure |
| **Minor NC** | Isolated failure; limited risk | 60 days for plan; 180 days for closure |
| **Observation** | Improvement opportunity; not a conformance failure | Next audit cycle |

---

## 4. Corrective Action Process

1. **Identification** — NC recorded in this register
2. **Containment** — Immediate risk mitigation (if required)
3. **Root Cause Analysis** — 5-Why or Fishbone analysis
4. **Corrective Action Plan** — Approved by DPO
5. **Implementation** — Actions completed by owner
6. **Effectiveness Verification** — DPO confirms NC cannot recur
7. **Closure** — NC formally closed with evidence

## 8. Escalation Rules

| Situation | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Major NC not remediated within 30 days | DPO escalates to CEO; weekly updates |
| NC involves potential regulatory breach | DPO + Legal assess notifiable breach |
| Recurring NC (same root cause) | Systemic review; PIMS framework assessment |
| NC from supplier | Supplier notified; assess data sharing suspension |
| NC from certification audit | Immediate DPO + senior management review |
