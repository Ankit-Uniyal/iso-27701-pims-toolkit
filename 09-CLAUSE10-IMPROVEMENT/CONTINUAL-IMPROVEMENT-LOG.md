## 1. Purpose

This log captures improvement opportunities and enhancement initiatives for the PIMS. It supports ISO/IEC 27701:2019 Clause 10.2 and demonstrates proactive improvement beyond mere compliance.

---

## 2. Difference from NCR Register

| Aspect | NCR Register | Continual Improvement Log |
|--------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Driver | Non-conformity; something went wrong | Proactive; something can be made better |
| Origin | Failure, incident, audit finding | Ideas, benchmarking, lessons learned |
| Urgency | Mandatory; response timelines apply | Voluntary; prioritised by benefit |
| Focus | Correcting the past | Improving the future |

---

## 3. Improvement Sources

Ideas may come from: Management review conclusions, staff suggestions, audit observations (not NCs), benchmarking, regulatory guidance, industry peer learning, post-incident lessons, data subject feedback, emerging privacy-enhancing technologies.

### IMP-2025-001: Role-Specific Privacy Training Modules

**Source:** Audit Observation | **Area:** Training | **Priority:** Medium

**Description:** Current training is generic for all staff. Role-specific modules for DPO, IT, Marketing, HR, Customer Service would increase relevance and retention.

**Expected Benefit:** Higher pass rates; reduced privacy incidents from staff error.

**Actions:**
1. Map role-specific risks per function (DPO + HR — 2025-03-31)
2. Develop 5 role-specific modules (DPO + HR — 2025-05-31)
3. Pilot with 2 departments, gather feedback (2025-06-15)
4. Full rollout (2025-06-30)

**Status:** Planned | **Target:** 2025-06-30

---

### IMP-2025-002: Automate ROPA Review Reminders

**Source:** Management Review | **Area:** ROPA | **Priority:** High

**Description:** ROPA entries reviewed manually with no automated reminders. Adding alerts will ensure no entries become outdated.

**Expected Benefit:** 100% ROPA currency; reduced DPO manual effort.

**Actions:**
1. Configure tracking tool to send 60-day review reminders (IT + DPO — 2025-02-28)
2. Update document control procedure to reference automation (DPO — 2025-03-31)

**Status:** In Progress | **Target:** 2025-03-31

---

### IMP-2025-005: Privacy by Design in IT Change Management

**Source:** Audit Observation | **Area:** Privacy by Design | **Priority:** High

**Description:** Privacy by Design checklist exists but not embedded in IT change management form. Changes can go live without mandatory DPO review gate.

**Expected Benefit:** 100% Art. 25 GDPR compliance; no systems go live without privacy review.

**Actions:**
1. Update IT change request form to include privacy screening (IT + DPO — 2025-03-15)
2. Configure change tool to require DPO sign-off for PII changes (IT — 2025-04-15)
3. Communicate to all IT project managers (2025-04-30)

**Status:** In Progress | **Target:** 2025-04-30
