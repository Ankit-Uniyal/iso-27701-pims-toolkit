## Purpose

This document defines the scope of [Organisation Name]'s Privacy Information Management System (PIMS), as required by ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Clause 4.3 (extension to ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 4.3). The PIMS scope must be documented, approved by top management, and communicated to relevant interested parties.
