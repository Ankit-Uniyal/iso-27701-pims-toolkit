## Purpose

This document defines [Organisation Name]'s Privacy Awareness Programme, establishing the approach to building and maintaining privacy awareness, understanding, and competence across the organisation. It supports ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Clauses 7.2 (Competence) and 7.3 (Awareness) (extensions to ISO/IEC 27001:2022).

---

## Programme Objectives

The Privacy Awareness Programme aims to:

1. Ensure all staff understand [Organisation Name]'s privacy obligations under ISO/IEC 27701:2025 and applicable regulations (GDPR, UAE PDPL, India DPDPA, etc.)
2. 2. Ensure staff can recognise and appropriately respond to privacy risks, including PII breaches
   3. 3. Build role-specific privacy competence for those with significant privacy responsibilities
      4. 4. Embed a culture of privacy accountability and Privacy by Design throughout the organisation

         6. ## 1. Target Audiences

         59. ## 6. Programme Review
        
         60. The Privacy Awareness Programme is reviewed annually by the DPO and HR, with output reported to the PIMS Management Review. Updates are made in response to:
