| Legal Counsel | Privacy law; SCCs; DPA drafting; regulatory enforcement | Advanced privacy law CPD | Annual |

| IT / Development | Privacy by design; secure coding; access controls | GDPR for developers; secure coding | Annual |
| Procurement | Processor due diligence; DPA requirements | Processor management and DPA | Annual |

| Senior Management / Board | Privacy governance; DPO authority; regulatory risk | Privacy for executives; board briefing | Annual |
