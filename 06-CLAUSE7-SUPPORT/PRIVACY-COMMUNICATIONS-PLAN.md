# Privacy Communications Plan

**Document ID:** PIMS-SUP-003
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2025 | **Owner:** DPO

---

## Internal Communications

| Communication | Audience | Channel | Frequency | Owner | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Privacy Policy update | All Staff | Email + intranet | Upon update | DPO | Notify of policy changes |
| Privacy KPI dashboard | Senior Management | Management report | Monthly | DPO | Report performance |
| Board privacy report | Board / ExCo | Board pack | Quarterly | DPO | Governance oversight |
| Privacy breach notification | Relevant staff | Email + meeting | Per incident | DPO | Incident awareness |
| New regulation update | Legal / Compliance | Email briefing | Upon change | DPO | Regulatory awareness |
| Privacy awareness campaign | All Staff | Intranet / email | Monthly themes | DPO / HR | Culture and awareness |
| DSAR updates | Customer-facing teams | Workshop | Annually | DPO | Operational guidance |
| Management review outputs | Senior Management | Meeting minutes | Annually | DPO | Governance outcomes |
| New processing activity brief | Affected teams | Team briefing | Per project | DPO | Privacy obligations |
| Privacy training reminders | All Staff | LMS + email | Monthly | HR / DPO | Training completion |

---

## External Communications

| Communication | Audience | Channel | Trigger | Owner | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Privacy Notice | Customers / visitors | Website; app | Upon collection / update | DPO | Transparency obligation |
| Breach notification (individuals) | Affected data subjects | Email / letter | High-risk breach | DPO | GDPR Art. 34 |
| DSAR acknowledgement | Data subject | Email / letter | Upon DSAR receipt | DPO | Confirm receipt; state deadline |
| DSAR response | Data subject | Email / secure portal | Within 1 calendar month | DPO | Legal right fulfilment |
| Breach notification (regulator) | ICO / UAE Data Office / DIFC | Online portal | Within 72 hours | DPO | GDPR Art. 33 |
| Regulatory enquiry response | Supervisory authority | Written | Per request | DPO / Legal | Regulatory cooperation |
| Processor communication | Third-party processors | Email + DPA | Onboarding + annually | DPO | Processor obligations |
| Cookie consent notification | Website visitors | Cookie banner | On website visit | IT / DPO | PECR compliance |
