## 1. Purpose

This dashboard defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and metrics used to monitor, measure, and report on the effectiveness of the Privacy Information Management System (PIMS). It supports ISO/IEC 27701:2019 Clause 9.1 (Monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation) and provides evidence for the Management Review.

---

## 2. KPI Reporting Framework

| Report Type | Frequency | Audience | Format |
|-------------|-----------|---------|--------|
| Operational KPI Summary | Monthly | DPO, CISO | Dashboard email/report |
| Privacy Metrics Report | Quarterly | PIMS Committee, Senior Management | Formal report |
| Management Review Input | Annual | Board, CEO, DPO | Management review deck |
| Regulatory Reporting | As required | Supervisory Authority | ICO/DPA submission |

## 6. Escalation Thresholds

| Scenario | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Any DSR not responded to within 25 days | Immediate DPO escalation; daily tracking |
| Any reportable breach | DPO and CISO notified immediately; ICO notification assessed |
| Critical risk open > 48 hours without treatment plan | Escalate to CEO and Board |
| Training completion < 85% at month-end | HR and DPO joint action plan |
| Supplier with missing DPA identified | Suspend data sharing; urgent DPA execution |
