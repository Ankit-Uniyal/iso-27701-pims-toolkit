# 15 — Supplementary Templates & Checklists

This folder contains standalone, reusable templates and checklists that support your PIMS implementation. These documents are ready to adapt and deploy — they complement the clause-specific documents in folders 01–13.

| 2 | [CONSENT-WITHDRAWAL-FORM-TEMPLATE.md](CONSENT-WITHDRAWAL-FORM-TEMPLATE.md) | Structured consent withdrawal form (GDPR Art. 7(3), ISO 27701 A.7.2.4) |

---

## When to Use This Folder

- **Pre-certification:** use the readiness checklist before your Stage 1/2 audit
- **Vendor management:** send the supplier questionnaire during procurement or contract renewal
- **Data subject interactions:** deploy the DSAR form and consent withdrawal form on your website or intranet
- **Contracting:** use the DPA template when appointing new data processors
- **Governance:** use the privacy governance templates for DPO appointments and board reporting
