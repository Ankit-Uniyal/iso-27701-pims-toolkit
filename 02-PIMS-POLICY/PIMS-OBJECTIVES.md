## Purpose

This document defines the measurable Privacy Objectives for [Organisation Name]'s Privacy Information Management System (PIMS), established in accordance with ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Clause 6.2 (extension to ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 6.2). Privacy objectives translate the Privacy Policy commitments into specific, measurable targets.

---

## Privacy Objectives (2025–2026)

| # | Objective | KPI / Measurement | Target | Owner | Review Frequency | Linked Control |
|---|-----------|-------------------|--------|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| 1 | Achieve and maintain full PII processing inventory coverage | % of processing activities documented in ROPA | 100% | DPO | Quarterly | A.7.2.1 |
| 2 | Respond to all Data Subject Access Requests within regulatory deadlines | % DSARs completed within 30 days (GDPR) / 5 days (UAE PDPL) | ≥ 95% | DPO | Monthly | A.7.3.2 |
| 3 | Ensure privacy awareness training completion across all staff | % of staff completing annual privacy training | ≥ 95% | DPO / HR | Annual | Clause 7.2 |
| 4 | Conduct DPIA for all high-risk processing activities before implementation | % of new high-risk processing activities with DPIA completed pre-launch | 100% | DPO | Per project | A.7.2.5 |
| 5 | Minimise PII breach notification delays | % of confirmed breaches notified to regulator within 72 hours (GDPR) | 100% | DPO | Per incident | A.7.3.3 |
| 6 | Maintain Statement of Applicability (SoA) currency | SoA reviewed and updated at least annually | Annually | DPO | Annual | Clause 6.1.3 |
| 7 | Embed Privacy by Design in all new systems and projects | % of new IT/business projects reviewed for Privacy by Design | 100% | DPO / IT | Per project | A.7.2.8, ISO 31700 |
| 8 | Assess all new third-party processors prior to engagement | % of new processors with completed due diligence before contract award | 100% | DPO / Procurement | Per engagement | A.7.5.2, B.8.5.1 |
| 9 | Achieve zero overdue corrective actions from internal PIMS audits | Number of overdue corrective actions (>90 days) | 0 | DPO | Quarterly | Clause 10.1 |
| 10 | Complete annual PIMS internal audit on schedule | Audit completed within planned window | Yes / No | DPO / Lead Auditor | Annual | Clause 9.2 |

## Objective Setting Criteria

Privacy objectives are set to be SMART:

- **Specific** — clearly defined scope and deliverable
- - **Measurable** — quantifiable KPI with defined target
  - - **Achievable** — realistic given available resources
    - - **Relevant** — linked to Privacy Policy, legal obligations, or risk treatment
      - - **Time-bound** — defined review frequency and deadline
