| **Use** | Complete this checklist before engaging a certification body for Stage 1 or Stage 2 audit |

---

## How to Use

Rate each item: **Y** = Yes / fully evidenced, **P** = Partial / in progress, **N** = No / not started.
A **Y** on all mandatory items (marked M) is required before Stage 2 audit. Partial items should be closed at least 4 weeks before audit date.
