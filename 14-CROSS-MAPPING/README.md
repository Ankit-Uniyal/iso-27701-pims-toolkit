# 14 — Cross-Mapping & Regulatory Alignment

This folder contains cross-reference guides mapping ISO/IEC 27701:2025 controls to major international privacy regulations and frameworks.

Use these documents **after** building your PIMS (folders 01–13) to verify alignment with your applicable legal jurisdiction(s).

## When to Use This Folder

- **Pre-certification:** confirm your PIMS controls satisfy the regulations that apply to your organisation
- **Multi-jurisdiction:** identify gaps when operating under more than one privacy law
- **Transition:** use the 2019 → 2025 guide if upgrading an existing certified PIMS
