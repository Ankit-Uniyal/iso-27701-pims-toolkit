## Appointment as Data Protection Officer

I am pleased to confirm your formal appointment as **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** of [Organisation Name] ("the Organisation") with effect from [Effective Date].

This letter sets out the terms of your appointment in accordance with **Article 37 of the UK/EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and **ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Clause 5.3**.

### 1. Scope of Appointment

You are appointed as DPO for the following legal entities and processing activities:
- [Organisation Name] (registered number [Company No.])
- [Subsidiary/affiliate names, if applicable]
- All personal data processing activities within the PIMS scope as defined in [PIMS Scope Statement reference]

### 2. Your Responsibilities

As DPO, you will:
- Inform and advise the Organisation and its employees about obligations under GDPR and applicable data protection laws
- Monitor compliance with GDPR, the UK Data Protection Act 2018, and ISO/IEC 27701:2025
- Oversee the PIMS, including risk assessments, DPIAs, and incident response
- Cooperate with and act as the primary contact for the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) and data subjects
- Provide advice on Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs) as required by GDPR Article 35

Your full responsibilities are set out in the DPO Terms of Reference ([reference: PIMS-LDR-002]).

### 3. Reporting Line

You will report directly to [CEO / Board / Audit Committee] and will have access to senior management without restriction. Your performance review will be conducted by [Name/Role].

### 4. Independence

In accordance with GDPR Article 38(3):
- You will not receive instructions from any person within the Organisation regarding the exercise of your DPO tasks
- You will not be dismissed or penalised for performing your DPO duties
- You will not undertake other tasks or duties that would result in a conflict of interest with your DPO role without prior written agreement from [CEO/Board]

### 5. Resources

The Organisation will provide:
- Dedicated time: [X days per week / full-time]
- Budget: [Annual budget for training, tools, external advice]
- Access to all relevant systems, databases, and personnel
- Membership of: [IAPP, BCS, or relevant professional body]

### 6. Confidentiality

You are required to maintain confidentiality of all personal data and commercially sensitive information accessed in the performance of your role, in accordance with your employment contract and the Organisation's confidentiality policy.

### 7. Publication

Your contact details as DPO will be published in the Organisation's Privacy Notice and on the ICO register (if required). Contact: [dpo@organisation.com]

### 8. Duration

This appointment is [permanent / for a fixed term of X years from [Effective Date]]. It may be terminated by either party giving [X months'] written notice, provided that such termination does not constitute a breach of GDPR Article 38(3).

Please sign and return the acceptance below to confirm your acceptance of this appointment.

Yours sincerely,

[CEO / Board Chair Name]
[Title]
[Organisation Name]
