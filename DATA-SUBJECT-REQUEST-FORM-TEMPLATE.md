## STAFF GUIDANCE — Processing DSR Forms

| Step | Action | Timeframe |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Log request in DSR tracking system; assign reference number | Day 0 — date received |
| 2 | Send acknowledgement to requestor with reference number and expected response date | Within 3 business days |
| 3 | Verify identity of requestor | Within 5 business days |
| 4 | If identity unclear, request additional verification (this pauses the clock) | Before day 10 |
| 5 | Search all relevant systems for the data subject's information | Within 20 days |
| 6 | Review any exemptions or partial restrictions (e.g., third-party data, legal privilege) | Within 25 days |
| 7 | Compile response and obtain DPO review | Within 28 days |
| 8 | Issue response to data subject | By day 30 (statutory deadline) |

**If extension required (complex case):** May extend by 2 additional months (total 3 months) — notify data subject within 30 days of original request, explaining reason.

**If request refused:** Notify data subject in writing with reasons; inform of right to complain to the ICO; no fee may be charged for refusal notification.
